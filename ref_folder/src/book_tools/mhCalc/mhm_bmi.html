Body Mass Index

The Body Mass Index relates height to weight and helps to determine ideal body proportions.

Input Weight  kg lbs
Input Height  cm in
 
 
Body Mass Index =

 

  Female Male
Underweight < 19 <20
Acceptable 19 - 25 20 - 25
Overweight 25 - 30 25 - 30
Obese 30 - 40 30 - 40
Morbidly Obese > 40 > 40

The formula is:

BMI = W / H2

where W is the weight in kilograms and H is the height in meters.

For more information on how the BMI should be interpreted, see the Centers for Disease Control and Prevention website.
