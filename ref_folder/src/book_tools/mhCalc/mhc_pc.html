The relationship of these measurements are:

1 mmHg = 1.36 cmH2O = 0.133 kPa = 0.0193 PSI

A Pascal (Pa) is a Newton-per-meter squared.

Please be aware of potential rounding errors when going back and forth between units of measurement.
